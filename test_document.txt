Employee Handbook - Tech Company Inc.

Regular work hours are Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

Base salary: $80,000 per year
Payment: Bi-weekly

- Health insurance (full coverage)
- Dental insurance
- 15 days vacation per year

Section 4: Notice Period
Employees must give 2 weeks notice before resignation.

Remote work is allowed 2 days per week with manager approval.
